Elisabeth Daem

Avenue des gloires nationales 34, 1083 Brussels Elisabeth.Daem@gmail.com

Elisabeth Daem (b. 1990, Brussels) is a commercial & IP law lawyer, with almost 8 years’ experience advising and assisting clients in various industries, however with a specific interest in the media and entertainment sector.

She practises in all main fields of general business law as well as in the field of IP law, handling (strategic) advisory, contractual and (complex) litigation work.

In addition, as a part-time assistant at the KULeuven (at the Centre for IT and IP), she regularly publishes legal articles and blogposts, speaks at seminars/webinars and is a member of a number of (local) industry associations such as BMM and ABA/BVA.

For legal advice, inquiries or assistance: elisabeth.daem@gmail.com
